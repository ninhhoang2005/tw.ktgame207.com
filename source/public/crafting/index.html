net

Last Modified: ago

Necessary materials

  • knife, for chopping wood
  • grass
  • wood

Item description

A fishing net. It can take more than one fish

Instructions

  1. Hold the grass and grass in your two hands, press on it. This will create a string, you need 400 strings.
  2. Hold the string and string in your two hands, press on it to form a tied_string, you need 80 tied_string.
  3. Holding tied_string with both your hands, press on it to form a heavily_tied_string. You need 16 heavily_tied_string.
  4. Hold heavily_tied_string with both of your hands, press on it to form a woven_string. You need 8 woven_string.
  5. Hold the woven_string with both of your hands, press on it to form a Heavily_woven_string. You need 4 Heavily_woven_string.
  6. Hold the Heavily_woven_string with both of your hands, press on it to form a Half_net. You need 2 Half_net.
  7. Hold Half_net in both your hands, press on it to form a Net_without_frame.
  8. Hold the wood in one hand and the knife in the other. Press on it to form a carved_wood. You need 6 carved_wood.
  9. Hold carved_wood in one of your hands, hold the knife in the other. Click on it to form a shaft. You need 4 shaft.
  10. Hold the shaft with both of your hands, press on it to form a long_shaft. You need 2 long_shaft.
  11. Hold long_shaft in one hand, carved_wood in the other. Press on it to create a half_frame. You need 2 half_frame.
  12. Hold half_frame with both of your hands. Press on it to create a frame.
  13. Hold frame in one of your hands, hold Net_without_frame in the other hand. Press on it to form a net.

cup

Last Modified: ago

Item Description

A cup for drinking water, coffee, and more.

Instructions

  • Step 1: Take a piece of paper and your free hand, then press it.

  • This will produce a folded piece of paper; repeat to make 2 folded pieces of paper.

  • Step 2: Take the 2 folded pieces of paper and put them together to create an unfinished cup.

  • Step 3: Take a paper cutter and use it to make a circular piece of paper.
