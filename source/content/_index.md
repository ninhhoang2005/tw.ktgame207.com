This is an official page for Terra Wilds
## Information



Name: Terra Wilds



Genre: Survival



Developer: Technology Entertainment Team



## Introduction



Terra Wilds is a combination of everyday life, adventure, and tons of challenges.



In this game, you can experience everything from being an explorer, to a skilled tradesperson if you have a solid understanding of maps, or simply an ordinary fisherman making a living day by day.



Everything depends on your decisions.



Terra Wilds has a diverse attribute system such as hunger, thirst, dirtiness, sickness, and many other factors.



## Interesting Things



*   Raffle system: This system allows people to spin for prizes, and those prizes are items, levels, or something random.

*   Map system: This game has many vast maps for people to explore and adventure through.

*   Item system: This game has many interesting and rare items waiting for you to hunt down.

*   Hunting system: Of course, a survival game must have a hunting system so people can kill animals and dissect them to eat raw to survive day by day.



And much more.
