Here we will tell you how to use the coffee machine

## coffee machine

coffee machine help you make coffee.

## how to use

To brew a cup of coffee, follow these steps:
1. Get the coffee machine: first press i to open the backpack, find the coffee machine, press the left - right arrows to hold it with the corresponding hand. Then press escape to get out of the backpack.
2. Place the coffee machine on the ground and activate: After taking the coffee machine out from the backpack, move to the hand holding the coffee machine (1 - left hand, 2 - right hand.), press space to place it on the ground and activate.
3. Put coffee or expresso into the machine: Press I to open the backpack, find coffee or expresso, press the arrow corresponding to the hand you want to hold it to take it out, press escape to exit the backpack. Move to the hand holding the coffee or expresso, press space to put it in the machine.
4. Put milk or water into the machine:
Put milk in the machine: Do the same as putting coffee in the machine but look for milk. After taking it out, put it in the machine like putting coffee in the machine.
Put water in the machine: If you don't want to use milk, you can use water instead. First press i to open the backpack, find the water bottle, press the arrow corresponding to the hand you want to take it out, press escape to exit the backpack. Then press t to open the water bottle, note: The other hand must not hold anything. Next, continue opening the backpack, find the open water bottle, take it out as above, press escape to exit the backpack. Put it in the machine like you put coffee in the machine.
5. Start making coffee: Press d to start making coffee, you will hear the sound of the coffee machine operating, wait until it finishes brewing (The sound of the coffee machine stops completely).
6. Take the coffee out of the machine: Open the backpack with i, find the cup, take it out, press escape to exit the backpack. Then switch to the hand holding the cup, press space to catch the coffee.
Note: You must have a cup to take coffee out of the machine. If you don't have a cup, your cup of coffee is considered wasted. Cups are items that must be crafted.
7. Put the coffee machine back in your backpack: Press enter to put the coffee machine back in your backpack. If you don't put the coffee machine back in your backpack, it will get dirty and you have to wait a while to be able to brew again. That's it, the coffee making is done.
Note, if you mix coffee with milk you will get a cup of milk coffee (cup off coffee with milk), if you mix coffee with water you will get a cup of black coffee (cup off coffee).
If you mix expresso with milk you will get a cup of lactate (cup off lactate), if you mix expresso with water you will get a cup of expresso (cup off expresso).
