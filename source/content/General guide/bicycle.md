## Item Description

bike: Can be used to quickly travel long distances, but easily drains energy.

## How to Use It

1.  Take the bike out of your inventory.
2.  Go to the hand holding the bike, and press Space to mount it.
3.  Press the Up arrow to go forward and the Down arrow to go backward.
4.  If you no longer want to ride the bike, press Alt+Backspace to dismount.

Note: The bike can only go straight. If you want to turn left, turn right, or turn around, you should change direction. For example, if I want to turn left, face west and then press the Up arrow to go straight.

Good luck!
